🍁 Adventure 12: Why Leaves Change Color!

Every autumn, leaves put on a beautiful colorful show. Let's find out how trees change their outfits!

Autumn Outfits

Trees that lose their leaves in the fall are called deciduous trees. As the weather cools down and days get shorter, these trees prepare for winter by resting. They stop making green chlorophyll, which reveals other hidden colors!

Did you know? Did you know? The red and purple colors in fall leaves are made from sugars trapped in the leaves when the nights get cold!

Nature's Paintboxes

Leaves contain different natural color paints called pigments. In spring, green is very strong. But in fall, orange, yellow, and red pigments get to shine. It's like the leaves are showing their true colors!

Keep exploring and asking questions to stay super smart! 🚀

🧠 Fun Quiz!

1. What do we call trees that lose their leaves every autumn?

🌳 We call them deciduous trees! They drop their leaves to save energy for winter.

2. What are the natural colors inside leaves called?

🎨 They are called pigments! When the green pigment fades, the yellow and orange pigments show up.
