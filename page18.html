💤 Adventure 18: Why We Dream!

When you close your eyes at night, your brain doesn't turn off. It goes to a secret dreamland!

Deep Sleep

When you are in a deep, peaceful sleep, we say you are in slumber. During slumber, your body heals itself, grows bigger, and rests its tired muscles so you feel fresh in the morning.

Did you know? Did you know? Everyone dreams every night, even if you don't remember them! Most dreams last between 5 and 20 minutes.

Talking Brain Cells

While you sleep, your brain cells, called neurons, are very busy. They sort through everything you learned during the day and mix up memories, creating funny, magical dreams!

Keep exploring and asking questions to stay super smart! 🚀

🧠 Fun Quiz!

1. What is another word for deep, peaceful sleep?

😴 It is called slumber!

2. What are the busy brain cells called that send messages while you sleep?

🧠 They are called neurons!
