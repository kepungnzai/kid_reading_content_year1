💨 Adventure 20: The Power of Wind!

Wind is just moving air. It can blow leaves, fly kites, and even make clean electricity!

Our Gas Blanket

Earth is wrapped in a thick blanket of air and gases called the atmosphere. When the sun heats up different parts of the atmosphere, the warm air rises and cool air rushes in, creating wind!

Did you know? Did you know? Wind power is clean and infinite! Unlike burning coal, wind doesn't make the air dirty and will never run out.

Giant Pinwheels

To catch the wind, humans build tall towers with giant spinning blades. This machine is a wind turbine. When the wind spins the blades, the turbine turns that movement into clean electricity for our homes!

Keep exploring and asking questions to stay super smart! 🚀
