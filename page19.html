🌙 Adventure 19: The Moon & Tides!

The moon is far away in space, but it has a powerful connection to our oceans. Let's see how!

Heavy Things Pull

Everything is made of matter. The amount of matter an object contains is its mass. The more mass an object has, the stronger its gravity pulls other things. Earth is very heavy, so its gravity pulls us down!

Did you know? Did you know? Because the Earth spins, the tides happen twice a day, creating high tide and low tide on beaches all over the world!

Ocean Waves

Even though the moon is smaller than Earth, its gravity pulls on our oceans. This pull creates tides, making the sea water rise and fall on the beach throughout the day!

Keep exploring and asking questions to stay super smart! 🚀

🧠 Fun Quiz!

1. What do we call the amount of matter inside an object?

2. What does the moon's gravity pull on Earth to create in our oceans?
