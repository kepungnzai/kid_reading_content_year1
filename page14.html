🪐 Adventure 14: The Solar System!

Our sun has a family of eight planets, lots of moons, and flying rocks. Let's take a tour!

Spinning Paths

Each planet travels around the sun in a curved path called an orbit. Gravity pulls the planets toward the sun, keeping them locked in their orbits so they don't float off into deep space!

Did you know? Did you know? Jupiter is so big that all the other planets in our solar system could fit inside it at the same time!

Space Boulders

Between Mars and Jupiter, there is a giant ring filled with rocky boulders called asteroids. Asteroids are left-over pieces from when the solar system formed. They look like lumpy potatoes floating in space!

Keep exploring and asking questions to stay super smart! 🚀

🧠 Fun Quiz!

1. What is the curved path called that planets take around the sun?

💫 It is called an orbit!

2. What do we call the rocky space boulders orbiting the sun?
