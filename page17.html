🦴 Adventure 17: How Bones Work!

Bones are hard, strong, and keep us standing tall. Let's see what is happening inside your bony body!

Your Body Frame

All 206 bones in your body join together to build a strong framework called a skeleton. Without a skeleton, you would be a floppy pile of jelly! Your skeleton also protects your soft brain and heart.

Did you know? Did you know? More than half of your bones are located in your hands, wrists, feet, and ankles!

Blood Factories

Bones are alive, and their center is soft! Inside many bones is a jelly-like substance called marrow. Marrow acts like a tiny factory, making millions of new red and white blood cells every single day!

Keep exploring and asking questions to stay super smart! 🚀

🧠 Fun Quiz!

1. What do we call the complete framework of bones inside your body?

💀 We call it your skeleton!

2. What is the soft jelly-like stuff inside bones that makes new blood cells?

🩸 It is called marrow!
