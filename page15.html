⭐ Adventure 15: How Stars Shine!

When you look up at night, the sky is full of tiny twinkling lights. But stars are actually giant, burning balls of gas!

Star Fuel

Stars are made mostly of a gas called hydrogen. Deep inside the star, hydrogen is squeezed so tightly that it turns into helium, releasing a massive amount of light and heat!

Did you know? Did you know? The sun is a star, and it is the closest star to Earth. It looks yellow and huge only because it is so close compared to other stars!

Connect-the-Dots

For thousands of years, people have connected the dots of stars to draw pictures in the night sky. A group of stars that forms a picture is called a constellation. Constellations can look like animals, hunters, or mythical creatures!

Keep exploring and asking questions to stay super smart! 🚀
