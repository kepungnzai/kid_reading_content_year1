🌱 Adventure 10: How Plants Eat!

Plants don't eat food like we do. Instead, they make their own food using sunlight, water, and air! Let's explore their green kitchen.

The Magic Green Paint

Leaves have a special green color inside them called chlorophyll. Chlorophyll acts like solar panels, catching sunlight and using its power to turn water and air into sugar for the plant to grow.

Did you know? Did you know? While plants cook their food, they clean our air! They take in gases we don't need and give out fresh air for us to breathe.

Clean Air for Everyone

During this cooking process, plants release a special gas called oxygen. Without plants making oxygen, there wouldn't be enough clean air for us to breathe. So, thank a tree today!

Keep exploring and asking questions to stay super smart! 🚀

🧠 Fun Quiz!

1. What is the green color inside leaves called that catches sunlight?

🍃 It is called chlorophyll! It acts like a tiny green solar panel.

2. What do plants release into the air that humans and animals need to breathe?
