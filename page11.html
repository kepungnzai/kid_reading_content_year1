⚡ Adventure 11: The Speed of Light!

Light travels so fast that it seems to happen instantly. But light is actually traveling at a super speed!

Super High Speed

The speed at which light moves is called its velocity. Light travels at an amazing velocity of 186,000 miles per second! It can travel around the Earth seven times in just one blink of your eye!

Did you know? Did you know? Sunlight takes about 8 minutes to travel from the sun to Earth. When you look at sunlight, you are seeing light that left the sun 8 minutes ago!

Outer Space Speedways

Light travels fastest in a vacuum, which is a place with no air, like outer space. Since there is no air to slow it down, light can zip through space without any speed bumps!

Keep exploring and asking questions to stay super smart! 🚀

🧠 Fun Quiz!

1. What word do scientists use to describe the speed of light?

🚀 Scientists call the speed and direction of light its velocity!

2. Where does light travel the fastest, where there is no air at all?

🌌 Light travels fastest in a vacuum, which is completely empty space!
