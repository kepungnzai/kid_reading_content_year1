🌎 Adventure 13: Earth's Layers!

The Earth we walk on is not solid rock all the way through. It has layers, just like an onion!

The Rocky Outer Shell

We live on the thin, rocky outer layer of the Earth called the crust. The crust holds the oceans and continents. It is like a giant eggshell covering the hot contents inside!

Did you know? Did you know? The temperature at the very center of the Earth is as hot as the surface of the sun — about 10,000 degrees Fahrenheit!

The Hot Rock Ocean

Right beneath the crust lies the mantle. The mantle is a thick layer of super hot rock that can flow very slowly, like thick molten plastic. It sits above the boiling metal core of the planet!

Keep exploring and asking questions to stay super smart! 🚀

1. What is the thin rocky outer layer of the Earth that we walk on?
