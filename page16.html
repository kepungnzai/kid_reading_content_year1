💧 Adventure 16: The Water Cycle!

Water on Earth is always moving and recycling. Let's see how water travels from the sky to the soil and back!

Falling from the Clouds

When clouds get too full, water falls back to Earth. This water is called precipitation. Precipitation can be liquid rain, soft snow, or icy hail, depending on how cold the air is!

Did you know? Did you know? The water you drink today is the exact same water that dinosaurs drank millions of years ago! It is recycled over and over.

Plant Sweating

Plants also release water into the air! This plant sweat is called transpiration. Water travels up the roots to the leaves, and escapes as vapor into the sky to make new clouds!

Keep exploring and asking questions to stay super smart! 🚀

🧠 Fun Quiz!

1. What is the science word for rain, snow, or hail falling from the sky?

🌧️ It is called precipitation!

2. What is it called when plants release water vapor through their leaves?

🌿 It is called transpiration!
