🦕 Adventure 3: Dino Land!

Millions of years ago, before humans ever walked the Earth, giant creatures called dinosaurs ruled the land. Let's travel back in time to meet them!

What Happened to the Dinosaurs?

Dinosaurs walked, swam, and flew all over the world for a very long time. But about 66 million years ago, a giant rock from space hit the Earth, changing the weather. Because of this, dinosaurs became extinct. This means they all died out, and there are no dinosaurs alive today!

Did you know? Not all dinosaurs were giant! Some dinosaurs, like the Microraptor, were as small as a modern crow and had feathers just like birds!

How Do We Know They Existed?

Since no human ever saw a living dinosaur, how do we know so much about them? We know because scientists find fossils in the ground! Fossils are ancient bones, teeth, leaf prints, or even footprints that have turned into rock over millions of years. By studying them, we can tell how big the dinosaurs were and what they liked to eat!

So, grab your explorer's shovel and magnifying glass, and keep searching for secrets of the past! 🔍

🧠 Fun Dinosaur Quiz!

1. What does it mean when we say dinosaurs are extinct?

☄️ It means that the entire group of animals died out, and there are none of them left alive on Earth today.

2. How do we know dinosaurs existed if no human ever saw one?

🦴 Because we find fossils (ancient bones, teeth, and footprints turned to stone) hidden in the ground!
